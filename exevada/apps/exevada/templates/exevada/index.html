Extreme event attribution catalogue

Our catalogue lists analyses of extreme events by the C3S prototype attribution service, and other attribution groups. The database contains detailed information on the events and their impacts, as well as numerical results of the statistical analyses the attribution statements are based on.

  • For analysed extreme events, go to the interactive events page.
  • See the attribution page for an overview of the attribution studies.
  • References to the observation time series and climate model datasets used by the attribution studies.
